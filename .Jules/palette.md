## 2024-05-24 - Accessible Star Ratings & Nested Interactive Elements
**Learning:** When adding interactive elements (like a wishlist button) inside clickable container elements (like the `ProductCard` div), `onClick={(e) => e.stopPropagation()}` is required to prevent unintended triggering of the parent container's click events. Also, visual star ratings should have the `aria-hidden="true"` attribute on their container and empty `alt` text (`alt=""`) on the individual star images to prevent redundant and confusing screen reader announcements, since the numeric rating is typically provided textually alongside them.
**Action:** Always add `onClick={(e) => e.stopPropagation()}` to buttons nested inside clickable cards. Always use `aria-hidden="true"` and empty `alt` text for visual star ratings that accompany a text-based rating.
